About MathMentor

MathMentor is a free AI-powered math tutoring platform built for middle and high school students. It was created to make quality math help accessible to every student, regardless of their access to private tutors or expensive test prep resources.

Why it exists

Most AI tools just give you answers. MathMentor is different — it's designed around how people actually learn math. Instead of handing you the solution, it walks through the reasoning step by step, asks follow-up questions to check your understanding, and generates practice problems to help you lock in each concept.

What makes it different

Three things: a guided explanation mode that never skips steps, a practice problem generator that creates targeted follow-up questions, and a progress tracker that shows which topics you've covered and which ones need more work.
