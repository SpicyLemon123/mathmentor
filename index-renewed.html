About MathMentor

MathMentor is a free AI-powered math tutoring platform built for middle and high school students. It was created to make quality math help accessible to every student, regardless of access to private tutors or expensive test prep resources.

Why it exists

Most AI tools just give you answers. MathMentor is different — it's designed around how people actually learn math. Instead of handing you the solution, it walks through the reasoning, asks follow-up questions to check understanding, and generates practice problems to lock in each concept.

What makes it different

Three things: a guided explanation mode that never skips steps, timed arithmetic drills for building fundamental fluency, and a topic library that gives students a clear path through any subject — from multiplication tables to AP Calculus.
